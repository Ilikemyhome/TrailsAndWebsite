What you might see along the Mount Storm King trail!

Keep an eye out for:

Wildlife

  • Black Bears
  • Mountain Lions
  • Birds
  • Elks and deer
  • Mountain Goats
  • If you're closer to the costal regions you might see: sea otters and seals

Vegitation

  • Madrona trees: These smooth, barkless trees are a highlight of the hike.
  • Evergreens: The Mount Storm King hike includes a damp forest with evergreens.
  • Other trees: The hike also includes manzanitas and tree roots.
